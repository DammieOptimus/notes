The Effectiveness and Influence of Telecommunications on the Present Generation and How It Can Help the Youth

Telecommunications, particularly through platforms like Top-Up and Get Reward (TGR), can significantly influence the present generation, especially the youth, by offering financial opportunities and leveraging their existing reliance on mobile technology.

Effectiveness and Influence of Telecommunications on the Present Generation

  • Financial Empowerment: TGR provides a platform for individuals to make money from telecommunication services they already use. Instead of just spending money on airtime, data, and bill payments, users can earn from these transactions.
  • Accessibility and Ease of Use: The TGR platform offers a virtual top-up (VTU) portal that allows users to conduct transactions from their phones. This eliminates the need to rely on third-party platforms, enabling users to make money from their own recharges and subscriptions.
  • Business Opportunity: TGR offers various registration packages, starting from a student package at ₦5,000. By becoming a member, individuals gain a license to operate their own VTU portal.
  • Referral and Commission System: TGR incentivises users to share the platform with others by offering referral commissions and transaction commissions. Users earn a percentage of the franchise fees when they register new members and also earn from their team members' transactions.
  • Incentives and Awards: TGR offers incentives such as travel funds, car awards, and house funds based on point value (PV) accumulation. These incentives can be earned relatively quickly, depending on the user's activity and network.

How Telecommunications Can Help the Youth

  • Financial Freedom: TGR provides an opportunity for young people to achieve financial freedom by building wealth through the Telecom sector. By creating a network of users, individuals can generate passive income from their transactions.
  • Skills Development: Engaging with the TGR platform can help young people develop digital marketing and networking skills. They can learn how to promote the platform, recruit new members, and manage their teams.
  • Wealth Creation: TGR allows the youth to tap into the lucrative telecommunications industry, where Nigerians spend significant amounts on airtime and data. By leveraging this existing market, young people can create wealth for themselves.
  • Economic Impact: With about 3 million Nigerians recharging their phones every hour and a yearly inflow investment of ₦21.4 trillion in the Telecoms Industry, TGR provides a means for the youth to benefit from this economic activity.
  • Overcoming Unemployment: For young people seeking job opportunities, TGR offers a platform to create income and become financially independent. The company provides support and training to help them succeed in the business.
  • Building a Sustainable Business: Unlike some network marketing businesses, TGR focuses on a product (telecommunications services) that is always in demand. This provides stability and sustainability for those building a business on the platform.

Specific Benefits and Features of TGR

  • VTU Portal: TGR provides a personalized VTU portal for members to carry out transactions. The portal allows users to recharge airtime, data, and pay for cable subscriptions and electricity bills.
  • Registration Packages: TGR offers various registration packages ranging from ₦5,000 to ₦100,000, each with different benefits and commission levels.
  • Referral Commissions: Users earn instant referral commissions when they register new members, with the amount varying based on the package the new member chooses.
  • Indirect Registration Bonuses: Users also earn indirect registration bonuses when their team members register new members. The depth of these bonuses depends on the user's own package.
  • Transaction Commissions: TGR members earn commissions on their team members' airtime and data purchases, as well as cable TV subscriptions and electricity bill payments.
  • Point Value (PV): PV is accumulated as users connect others to their VTU portal and as their team members connect their own people. PV is used to qualify for various incentives and awards.
  • Leadership Incentives: TGR offers leadership incentives for members who generate a certain amount of PV each month, as well as incentives for reaching milestones in PV accumulation.
  • Training and Support: TGR provides training and support to help members grow their businesses. This includes access to training groups where members can learn ways to grow their business.
